About Sakshi Sethiya

Sakshi is a dedicated psychologist with a passion for helping individuals navigate life's challenges. Her approach combines evidence-based techniques with genuine empathy, creating a safe and supportive space for growth and healing.

Specializing in anxiety, depression, relationships, stress management, Sakshi empowers clients to build resilience, understand themselves better, and cultivate lasting positive change.
